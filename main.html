"We see a proliferation of new conflicts; old conflicts seem never to die. Conflicts are becoming more and more interlinked, more linked with the new threat of global terrorism. In many situations they are internal conflicts, asymmetric, with terrible violations of international humanitarian law and human rights law. They are the result of fragilities of states, of institutions, of societies, and in a world where power relations became unclear not only unpredictability and impunity tend to proliferate, but it is very difficult for the international community to prevent crises and to timely solve them."

- Antonio Guterres, Secretary-General of the United Nations
